# Week 16 – Capstone Project & Career Prep

## 🎯 Objectives
- Demonstrate mastery of all skills from Weeks 1–15
- Deliver a polished, professional Power BI report
- Present findings with a short written analysis
- Prepare for a data analytics career at FNB and beyond

---

## 🏆 Capstone Options

Choose **one** of the following project tracks:

---

### Option 1 – Sales Analytics Dashboard
**Dataset:** Financial Sample or AdventureWorks (built into Power BI)

**Requirements:**
- [ ] 2-page report: Executive Summary + Regional Detail
- [ ] Measures: Total Sales, YoY Growth, Profit Margin %, Top 5 Products
- [ ] Slicers: Year, Region, Product Category
- [ ] KPI visual showing target vs actual
- [ ] What-if parameter: Price increase %

---

### Option 2 – Customer Segmentation Report
**Dataset:** `data/Customer-Segmentation.csv`

**Requirements:**
- [ ] Segment customers into Low / Medium / High value (DAX calculated column)
- [ ] 2-page report: Segment Overview + Customer Detail
- [ ] Measures: Customers per Segment, Avg Spend, Total Revenue by Segment
- [ ] Key Influencers visual (what drives High spender classification)
- [ ] Slicer: Region, Segment

---

### Option 3 – Operations Monitoring Dashboard
**Dataset:** Custom / self-sourced or create a simulated operations dataset

**Requirements:**
- [ ] Daily and weekly KPI tracking
- [ ] 2-page report: KPI Overview + Trend Analysis
- [ ] Measures: Daily Average, Weekly Total, % Change WoW
- [ ] Line chart for trends, cards for KPIs
- [ ] Date slicer and navigation buttons

---

## 📝 Written Report (Short Write-up)

Along with your `.pbix`, submit a short write-up (1–2 pages) covering:

1. **Project Summary** – What dataset did you use? What business question did you answer?
2. **Data Preparation** – What cleaning/transformation steps did you apply in Power Query?
3. **DAX Measures** – List all measures created with brief descriptions
4. **Key Insights** – What 3 insights did your report reveal?
5. **Challenges & Learnings** – What was difficult? What did you learn?
6. **SFIA Skills Applied** – Map your work to DAAN, DENG, VISL, DATM

---

## 🎤 Presentation Guidelines

- Duration: **10–15 minutes** per group
- Walk through your `.pbix` live (or use screenshots)
- Each group member should present at least one section
- Be prepared to answer questions about your DAX measures and design choices
